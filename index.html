Who We Are

The University of Toronto Mississauga Mental Health Student Association (UTM MHSA) was founded in 2021 to support and empower students on their mental health journeys. We connect students with resources, host meaningful events, and foster a supportive community where mental wellness is prioritized.

Community Events

Mental Health Advocacy
