About the Program

The goal of the MHSA Mentorship Program is to foster meaningful relationships between upper-year mentors and mentees while supporting the mental health and well-being of students across campus.

Through open conversations, shared experiences, and mutual support, we work to destigmatize mental health and create a safe space for students to grow, reflect, and overcome challenges together.
